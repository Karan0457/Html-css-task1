Goku's Journey: Achieving Legendary Powers

Follow Goku's path from a young boy to a powerful Saiyan warrior!

1. Goku as a Young Boy (The Beginning)

Goku started his journey as a small, innocent boy living in the mountains. He was raised by Grandpa Gohan, who taught him basic martial arts and how to survive in the wild. Although Goku was strong even as a child, he was unaware of his Saiyan heritage.

2. Goku's First Major Transformation: The Great Ape (Oozaru)

When Goku first encountered the full moon, he transformed into a giant Great Ape (Oozaru) during the battle with the Saiyan warrior, Raditz. This transformation is a special ability that all Saiyans possess, which boosts their strength drastically, though Goku was unable to control it at the time.

3. The Rise of the Super Saiyan: Goku's First Transformation

Goku's first major breakthrough came during his battle with Frieza on Planet Namek. In an intense moment of rage after the death of his best friend, Krillin, Goku unlocked the Super Saiyan transformation. His hair turned golden, his eyes became teal, and his power surged to an unimaginable level. This transformation marked the beginning of Goku's rise as one of the universe's most powerful warriors.

4. Super Saiyan 2: Goku's Next Step

Years later, Goku continued to grow stronger, reaching the level of Super Saiyan 2. This transformation came during the Cell Games, when Goku passed on the responsibility of defeating Cell to his son Gohan. Super Saiyan 2 gave Goku even more power and control, and his aura was surrounded by electricity, signaling this advanced form.

5. Super Saiyan 3: Goku's Most Powerful Form (At the Time)

Goku's power reached new heights with the Super Saiyan 3 transformation. This form drastically increased his strength, speed, and energy projection abilities, though it drained his stamina quickly. Goku used this form to fight against Majin Buu, showcasing how far he had come since his childhood.

6. Super Saiyan God: Goku's Godly Power

During the Battle of Gods saga, Goku unlocked the Super Saiyan God transformation. This form was achieved by combining the power of five pure-hearted Saiyans, and it granted Goku god-like powers. His hair turned red, and his aura became fiery. With this new power, Goku was able to fight the mighty Beerus, the God of Destruction.

7. Super Saiyan Blue: Combining God Power with Super Saiyan

Goku's power grew even more with the Super Saiyan Blue transformation, a combination of Super Saiyan and Super Saiyan God. With blue hair and a calm, controlled aura, Super Saiyan Blue allowed Goku to harness the divine energy of Super Saiyan God while maintaining the incredible speed and power of Super Saiyan.

8. Ultra Instinct: Mastery of Combat

Finally, Goku achieved Ultra Instinct, a state where his body moves without thinking, allowing him to react to attacks with near-perfect reflexes. This form is one of the highest levels of power in the Dragon Ball universe, and it requires complete mastery over one's body and mind. Goku unlocked this ability during his battle with Jiren in the Tournament of Power.
